Mobile Technology

Frequency • Context • Speed • Cost

  • Frequency: customers interact more often
  • Context: business knows the customer situation (Location/Time/Device/Intent)
  • Speed: decisions + service happen in real time
  • Cost: self-service + automation reduce cost-to-serve

1) Frequency = More Touchpoints

  • Meaning: Micro-interactions happen daily (not monthly).
  • Business effect: Habit → loyalty → repeat purchases.
  • Examples: UPI payments, food re-ordering, bill payments.
  • KPIs: repeat rate ↑, churn ↓, LTV ↑.

2) Context = Situation-Aware Service

  • Meaning: Mobile captures location, time, intent, usage signals.
  • Business effect: Right offer/service at the right moment.
  • Examples: “near you + open now”, map routing, due-date reminders.
  • KPIs: conversion ↑, marketing waste ↓, service errors ↓.

3) Speed = Real-Time Response

  • Meaning: Instant booking, instant payment, instant updates.
  • Business effect: Less waiting → fewer drop-offs.
  • Examples: Ola booking, UPI success/failure, live order status.
  • KPIs: conversion ↑, time-to-serve ↓, complaints ↓.

4) Cost = Self-Service + Automation

  • Meaning: Customers do tasks in-app; automation reduces manual work.
  • Business effect: Serve more customers with the same staff.
  • Examples: digital KYC, WhatsApp support bots, mobile boarding pass.
  • KPIs: cost-to-serve ↓, fewer calls/visits, margin ↑.
